Quiz App - Updates and Usage Guide

App Updates

Version 1.0.1 - August 19, 2024

Initial release of the Quiz App. Features include multiple-choice questions, score tracking, and a user-friendly interface.

How to Use the Quiz App

1. Starting the Quiz

When you open the app, you'll see a welcome screen. Tap the "Start Quiz" button to begin answering questions.

2. Answering Questions

Each question will be presented with multiple choice answers. Select the answer you think is correct by tapping on it. The app will automatically move to the next question after you make a selection.

3. Tracking Progress

At the top of the screen, you'll see your progress through the quiz, indicating which question you're on out of the total number of questions.

4. Completing the Quiz

After answering all questions, you'll be taken to a results screen where you can see your score.

5. Reviewing Answers

On the results screen, you can review which questions you answered correctly and incorrectly. This feature helps you learn and improve for future quizzes.

6. Retaking the Quiz

If you want to try again, there's a "Restart Quiz" button on the results screen that will take you back to the beginning with a fresh set of questions.

Tips for Using the App

  • Read each question carefully before selecting an answer.
  • Use the progress indicator to pace yourself through the quiz.
  • After completing the quiz, review your incorrect answers to learn and improve.
  • Try taking the quiz multiple times to familiarize yourself with different questions and improve your knowledge.
