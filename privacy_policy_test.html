Privacy Policy for Quiz App

Last updated: August 20, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to our Quiz App, a fun and engaging way to test your general knowledge across various topics. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing an enjoyable experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information, ensuring transparency in all our practices.

Local Data Storage: Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Our Quiz App is designed to operate entirely offline, with all quiz questions and content stored locally on your device. This approach offers several key advantages:

  • Enhanced Privacy: By storing questions locally, we eliminate the need for any server connections or data transmission, ensuring your usage remains completely private.
  • Offline Functionality: You can enjoy our quiz anytime, anywhere, without requiring an internet connection.
  • Improved Performance: Local storage means faster load times and a smoother user experience, free from server-related delays.
  • Data Security: Your activity and progress are stored only on your device, minimizing the risk of data breaches or unauthorized access.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information, ensuring complete user anonymity.

No Personal Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to, names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

Data Storage and Security

All quiz content is securely stored in the app's local assets folder on your device. We have implemented measures to ensure this data remains secure and protected from unauthorized access.

No Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services, such as analytics tools, advertising networks, or social media platforms. Your activity within the app remains private and solely on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not collect any information that could identify or track users, ensuring a safe environment for children to use the app.

Updates and Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Any changes will be documented in this policy and made available within the app. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy periodically.
